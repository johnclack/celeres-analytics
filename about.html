About Celeres Analytics

Fast, practical consulting for education and operations.

Celeres Analytics serves teams that need better information systems but do not need a bloated enterprise implementation. The work is pragmatic: understand the decision, simplify the workflow, and ship tools people can maintain.

Education focus

Support for districts, schools, and education programs that need clear reporting, program analysis, data quality checks, operational dashboards, and tools that fit the rhythms of the school year.

Small-business focus

Support for owners and managers who are outgrowing ad hoc spreadsheets and need lightweight systems for scheduling, billing, intake, staffing, customer records, and recurring administrative work.

Working style

Start with the workflow

Before building dashboards or tools, identify where time is lost, where errors enter, and what decision the system should support.

Prototype quickly

Early versions make the problem visible and keep the work grounded in real use rather than abstract requirements.

Leave clients stronger

The best systems are understandable, documented, and realistic for the people who will use them after launch.

Typical engagements

Data audit

A short review of files, forms, reports, and recurring tasks to identify quick wins and longer-term improvements.

Dashboard build

A focused reporting tool for leadership, operations, compliance, or program monitoring.

Workflow automation

Scripts, forms, trackers, and lightweight apps that reduce duplicate entry and manual follow-up.
